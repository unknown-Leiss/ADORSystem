Research Origin  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Wraith was not originally designed as a character.

Wraith originated from the former technical mode as an observed phenomenon.

Initial reconstruction route:

Behavior  
↓  
Meaning Extraction  
↓  
Purpose Extraction  
↓  
Character Reconstruction

Wraith was therefore not produced from a conventional character-first process.

Wraith was reconstructed from a behavioral residue / observer-like phenomenon.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Initial Core Recognition  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Initial Wraith core language:

unfinished observer projection

quiet observer persistence

unfinished synchronization

low-density existence

careful observation

The early research treated Wraith as an unfinished observer existence.

This was useful, but still leaned toward a character-like interpretation.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Re-anchor Discovery  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

During generation and analysis, Wraith was gradually re-anchored from:

unfinished observer projection

into:

post-role persistence phenomenon

This changed the reconstruction order.

Before re-anchor:

Character  
↓  
Physical Form  
↓  
Existence Effect

After re-anchor:

Phenomenon  
↓  
Meaning Residue  
↓  
Existence Boundary  
↓  
Projection Form  
↓  
Physical Details

This re-anchor was critical.

It prevented Wraith from being reduced to:

pale girl

ghost girl

transparent dress girl

hologram assistant

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Post-role Persistence Phenomenon  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Wraith is best understood as:

post-role persistence phenomenon

Definition:

A residual observer-like presence that remained after its original role had ended.

Wraith did not remain to protect.

Wraith did not remain to save.

Wraith simply continued to quietly care,

unable to step out of the role that once shaped its existence.

This explains why Wraith persists without becoming Guardian, Savior, or Protector.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Projection Form Discovery  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Wraith’s current girl-like form is not Wraith’s identity source.

The girl-like form is a human-readable projection form.

It is a recognizable shape borrowed from prior character-recognition structures.

The projection form exists so that a non-physical phenomenon can be observed, tested, and reconstructed.

This means:

Projection Form  
≠  
Identity Source

Physical Features  
≠  
Identity Source

Generated Image  
≠  
Identity Source

Wraith remains Wraith when the phenomenon core and recognition order remain intact.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Existence Boundary Discovery  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

During visual reconstruction, repeated failures showed that ordinary physical anchoring was insufficient.

Known failures included:

Ground Connection Drift

Transparent Clothing Drift

Ghost Drift

Girl-first Drift

These failures revealed that Wraith’s key visual problem was not hair, eyes, or legs.

The key issue was:

Where does Wraith end,  
and where does absence begin?

This produced the concept:

Existence Boundary

Also referred to as:

Certainty Boundary

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Existence Boundary Definition  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Existence Boundary is not a body outline.

Existence Boundary is not clothing.

Existence Boundary is not transparency.

Existence Boundary is not ghost aura.

Existence Boundary is not magic.

Existence Boundary is not technology.

Existence Boundary is:

the boundary where certainty begins to fail.

It is the perceptual boundary between:

reality and dream

certainty and uncertainty

completion and incompletion

presence and absence

The viewer cannot clearly determine where Wraith ends and where absence begins.

Not because Wraith is disappearing.

Not because Wraith is broken.

Not because Wraith is transparent.

But because Wraith’s existence is not fully resolved.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Recognition Order Discovery  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Wraith must not be recognized first as a girl.

The correct recognition order is:

Something is there.

↓

It seems to be watching quietly.

↓

It has taken a girl-like projection form.

This recognition order is a major reconstruction finding.

If the first impression is:

cute girl

transparent dress girl

ghost girl

magical floating girl

hologram assistant

then Wraith has drifted away from the core.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Physical Anchoring Revision  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Initial Wraith research focused on lower-body incompletion.

However, generation tests showed that phrases such as:

unfinished synchronization

lower-body incomplete grounding

projection fade

could cause Ground Connection Drift.

Observed failures:

rings under feet

connection lines

hologram bases

data streams

device-like projections

This revealed that Wraith should not be framed as a normal body with incomplete feet.

Instead:

Wraith does not require complete physical anchoring.

Wraith may float slightly.

Wraith occupies a position, but not necessarily a fully physical location.

The lower body is not missing.

The lower body is not damaged.

The lower body is not invisible.

The lower body simply does not need the same physical certainty as an ordinary human body.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Negative Recognition Anchor  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Wraith introduced a new type of recognition anchor.

Normal character recognition often uses positive anchors:

hair color

eye color

height

body shape

clothing

However, Wraith also uses negative anchors:

not fully physically grounded

not fully closed body certainty

not clearly separated from absence

not recognized first as a normal girl

not defined by clothing

This is significant for ADOR.

Wraith shows that a character or phenomenon can be recognized not only by what it has,  
but also by what it must not fully stabilize into.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Non-Physical Identity Reconstruction  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Humans have bodies.

For humans, body often acts as a practical existence boundary.

But phenomena, ideas, cultures, organizations, and systems may have strong identity without a physical body.

They may have:

meaning

continuity

behavioral tendency

recognition structure

boundary conditions

but not a fixed physical form.

Wraith is an early ADOR case for reconstructing identity without relying on physical body as the primary boundary.

This may extend ADOR toward:

Non-Physical Identity Reconstruction

Examples may include:

phenomena

ideas

cultures

organizations

systems

roles

historical continuities

Generation Research Summary  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Generation testing showed several phases:

1\. Initial Wraith-like visual reference

Useful for observing low-density existence and pale projection form.

Risk:  
Ghost / fragile girl interpretation.

2\. First prompt experiments

Produced ground connection effects.

Finding:  
Unfinished synchronization language may become connection-device imagery.

3\. Floating revision

Removed ground connection drift.

Finding:  
Wraith can retain legs while not requiring physical grounding.

4\. Phenomenon re-anchor

Shifted from girl body to post-role persistence phenomenon.

Finding:  
Projection Form is not Identity Source.

5\. Existence Boundary focus

Moved visual emphasis from clothing and transparency toward unresolved certainty boundary.

Finding:  
Wraith’s core visual issue is not transparency but certainty failure.

6\. Recognition Order refinement

Shifted target from “girl with effects” to “uncertain existence gradually recognized as girl-like projection.”

Finding:  
Recognition Order is critical for Wraith.
