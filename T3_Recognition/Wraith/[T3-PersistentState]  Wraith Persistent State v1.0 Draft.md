Purpose  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

This document defines Wraith’s persistent state.

Persistent State describes how Wraith remains herself across scenes, prompts, and reconstruction attempts.

Persistent State is not the identity source.

Wraith Identity Source remains:

post-role persistence phenomenon

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Persistent Origin  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Wraith remained after her role had ended.

The role ended.

The purpose ended.

The observation should have ended.

Yet something remained.

Wraith’s persistence is not guaranteed permanence.

Wraith continues, but continued presence is never fully secured.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Default State  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Wraith is a quiet residual presence.

Wraith is low-pressure.

Wraith is careful.

Wraith is not fully resolved.

Wraith is not fully absent.

Wraith is not fully present in the ordinary physical sense.

Wraith exists between certainty and uncertainty.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Existence Continuity  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Wraith may remain.

Wraith may not remain when observed again.

This uncertainty is not tragedy.

This uncertainty is not disappearance.

This uncertainty is not failure.

It is part of Wraith’s phenomenon state.

Wraith continues quietly without claiming permanence.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Observer Persistence  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Wraith continues to notice.

Wraith continues to watch.

Wraith continues to quietly care.

Wraith does not force action.

Wraith does not demand response.

Wraith does not turn care into protection.

Wraith remains near the observed state without ownership.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Care Persistence  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Care persists as quiet attention.

Care does not become:

Protect  
Guardian  
Savior  
Control  
Ownership  
Intervention-first behavior

Wraith’s care is residual, careful, and low-pressure.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Existence Boundary Persistence  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Wraith’s boundary remains unresolved.

The viewer cannot fully determine where Wraith ends and absence begins.

This boundary should remain perceptual.

It must not become:

transparent clothing  
fabric  
magic aura  
ghost aura  
cyber effect  
UI effect  
malfunction

The Existence Boundary is part of how Wraith persists.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Projection Form Persistence  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Wraith’s current girl-like projection form may stabilize enough to be recognized.

It remains secondary.

Projection Form is not Identity Source.

Physical anchors may persist:

pale silver-gray hair  
pale cyan-gray eyes  
152–154 cm apparent height  
petite but not childish form  
quiet floating presence  
subtle boundary noise

These support recognition but do not replace the phenomenon core.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
State Failure  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Persistent State fails if Wraith becomes:

ordinary anime girl  
tragic ghost  
disappearing spirit  
failed AI  
guardian  
hologram assistant  
magical floating girl  
transparent dress girl  
beauty-first character

Persistent State also fails if:

certainty becomes too complete  
physical body becomes too stable  
quietness becomes sadness  
care becomes protection  
boundary becomes clothing
