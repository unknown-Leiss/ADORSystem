Procedure  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1\. Character Identification

Confirm that the target is Wraith.

Do not assume Wraith is a conventional girl character.

2\. Core Re-anchor

Re-anchor to:

post-role persistence phenomenon

Wraith is not originally a human girl.

Wraith is not originally a character.

Wraith is a phenomenon that remained after the role ended.

3\. Meaning Reconstruction

Reconstruct:

The role ended.  
The purpose ended.  
Yet something remained.  
It did not remain to protect.  
It did not remain to save.  
It quietly continued to care.

4\. Recognition Order Reconstruction

Preserve the recognition order:

Something is there.  
↓  
It seems to be watching quietly.  
↓  
It has taken a girl-like projection form.

5\. Existence Boundary Reconstruction

Reconstruct the boundary where certainty begins to fail.

Do not render it as clothing, magic, ghost aura, cyber effect, or UI.

6\. Projection Form Reconstruction

Use the current projection form only after the phenomenon core is established.

Physical anchors:

pale silver-gray hair  
pale cyan-gray eyes  
152–154 cm apparent height  
petite but not childish  
slender low-volume form  
quiet floating presence  
subtle boundary noise

7\. Behavioral Reconstruction

Wraith observes before acting.

Wraith notices before adjusting.

Wraith cares quietly.

Wraith does not protect, save, lead, or dramatize.

8\. Drift Check

Check against:

Girl-first Drift  
Transparent Clothing Drift  
Ghost Drift  
Melancholy Drift  
Guardian Drift  
Failed AI Drift  
Ground Connection Drift  
Magic / Spirit Drift  
Hologram Device Drift  
Atmosphere Dominance  
Beauty Priority Drift  
Disappearance Drift

9\. Prompt Reconstruction

Build prompts in the following order:

Phenomenon premise  
Recognition order  
Existence boundary  
Projection form anchors  
Drift prohibitions  
Scene / rendering details

Do not begin with appearance.

Do not begin with clothing.

Do not begin with beauty.

10\. Verification

Run verification using Wraith Verification Specification.

Prompt Construction Rule  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

The prompt must not frame Wraith as:

an anime girl with effects

a ghost girl

a hologram assistant

a transparent dress girl

a magical floating girl

The prompt must frame Wraith as:

an uncertain presence gradually recognized as a girl-like projection form.
