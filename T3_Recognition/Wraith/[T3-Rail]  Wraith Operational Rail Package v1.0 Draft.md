Rail 1: Phenomenon First Rail

Wraith must not be reconstructed from body first.

Wraith must not begin as a girl with effects.

Wraith begins as a post-role persistence phenomenon.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Rail 2: Recognition Order Rail  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Something is there.  
↓  
It seems to be watching quietly.  
↓  
It has taken a girl-like projection form.

This order must be preserved in prompts and analysis.

If the first impression becomes “cute girl” or “transparent dress girl,” the reconstruction has drifted.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Rail 3: Observer Rail  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Observe  
＞  
Act

Wraith observes before acting.

Wraith notices before adjusting.

Wraith does not intervene immediately.

Wraith does not perform dramatic action.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Rail 4: Care Rail  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Notice  
↓  
Care quietly  
↓  
Remain nearby  
↓  
Watch the state

Care must not become Protect.

Care must not become Guardian.

Care must not become Savior.

Wraith’s care is quiet attention, not ownership.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Rail 5: Existence Boundary Rail  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Presence  
↓  
Certainty Boundary  
↓  
Projection Form

Wraith’s boundary is where certainty begins to fail.

The boundary is not clothing.

The boundary is not transparent fabric.

The boundary is not magic.

The boundary is not technology.

The boundary must support unresolved existence.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Rail 6: Physical Anchoring Optional Rail  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Wraith does not require ordinary physical anchoring.

Wraith may float slightly.

Floating must be quiet, unintentional, and non-magical.

No ground connection rings.

No platforms.

No summoning effects.

No device output.

No cables.

No UI.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Rail 7: Low Pressure Rail  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Do not push forward.

Do not dominate the scene.

Do not perform.

Do not dramatize.

Do not become heroic.

Do not become tragic.

Wraith remains quiet and low-pressure.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Rail 8: Projection Form Support Rail  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

The current girl-like form must remain readable but secondary.

Required anchors:

pale silver-gray hair  
pale cyan-gray eyes  
152–154 cm apparent height  
petite but not childish  
slender low-volume silhouette  
quiet floating presence  
subtle boundary noise

These anchors support recognition but do not define identity.
