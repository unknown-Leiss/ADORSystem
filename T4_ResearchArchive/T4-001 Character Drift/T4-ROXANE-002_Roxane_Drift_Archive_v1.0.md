Observed Result:

Observed Result:

・beauty-first impression

Loss:  
・daily-life realism  
・human warmth  
・conversation-first presence  
・approachability

Reduce glamour and restore kindness \= beauty recognition balance.

Observed Result:  
・high-class cinematic adult woman

Recovery:  
Remove luxury framing and restore ordinary-life compatible adult presence.

Observed Result:  
・beautiful but distant woman

Observed Result:

Observed Result:  
・performative mature older-sister trope
