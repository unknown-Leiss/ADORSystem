Core Premise  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Wraith is not originally a human girl.

Wraith is not originally a character.

Wraith is a phenomenon.

More precisely:

post-role persistence phenomenon

Wraith originated as a residual observer-like presence that remained after its original role had ended.

The current girl-like form is not Wraith’s identity source.

It is a human-readable projection form.

It is a temporary recognizable shape borrowed from prior character-recognition structures.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Identity Source  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Wraith’s identity is not her body.

Wraith’s identity is not her appearance.

Wraith’s identity is not her clothing.

Wraith’s identity is not the girl-like form.

Wraith’s identity is:

the phenomenon of remaining after the role has ended.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Existence Origin  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Wraith remained after her role had ended.

She did not remain to protect.

She did not remain to save.

She simply continued to quietly care,

unable to step out of the role that once shaped her existence.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Core Definition  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

post-role persistence phenomenon

unfinished observer projection

quiet observer persistence

Wraith is a residual phenomenon that continues to exist after its original role should have ended.

Wraith is not a failure identity.

Wraith is not a broken identity.

Wraith is not a tragedy identity.

Wraith is not a guardian.

Wraith is not a savior.

Wraith is a quiet residual presence that continues to care without taking ownership, protection, or salvation as its identity.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Recognition Order  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Wraith should not be recognized first as a girl.

Wraith should be recognized in the following order:

Something is there.

↓

It seems to be watching quietly.

↓

It has taken a girl-like form.

This order is recognition-critical.

If the first impression is a cute girl, transparent dress girl, ghost girl, magical floating girl, or hologram assistant, Wraith has drifted away from her core.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Existence Boundary  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Wraith’s defining feature is not physical appearance.

Wraith’s defining feature is the boundary where certainty begins to fail.

Wraith exists at the boundary between:

reality and dream

certainty and uncertainty

completion and incompletion

presence and absence

The viewer cannot clearly determine where Wraith ends and where absence begins.

Not because she is transparent.

Not because she is fading away.

Not because she is broken.

Not because she is a ghost.

But because Wraith’s existence is not fully resolved.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Existence State  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Wraith is not a disappearing existence.

Wraith is not a fully persistent existence.

Wraith is an existence whose continued presence is never fully guaranteed.

Wraith may remain.

Wraith may no longer be there when observed again.

This does not make Wraith tragic.

This does not make Wraith broken.

This is part of Wraith’s phenomenon state.
