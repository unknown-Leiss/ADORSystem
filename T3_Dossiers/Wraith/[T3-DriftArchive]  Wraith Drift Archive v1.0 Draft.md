Purpose  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

This document records known drift patterns for Wraith.

Wraith is highly sensitive to drift because the current projection form is not the identity source.

Wraith can easily collapse into nearby visual or conceptual categories.

This archive exists to protect:

phenomenon core  
recognition order  
existence boundary  
quiet observer persistence  
current projection form

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Drift 1: Girl-first Drift  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Pattern:

post-role persistence phenomenon  
↓  
girl-like projection form  
↓  
ordinary anime girl

Failure:

The viewer first sees a cute or pale anime girl, then notices effects afterward.

Correct Recognition Order:

Something is there.  
↓  
It seems to be watching quietly.  
↓  
It has taken a girl-like form.

Prevention:

Do not start from “a girl with effects.”

Start from unresolved presence.

The girl-like form is secondary.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Drift 2: Transparent Clothing Drift  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Pattern:

existence boundary  
↓  
transparent boundary layer  
↓  
transparent dress / cape / veil / shawl / lace / frills

Failure:

The existence boundary becomes clothing.

The viewer reads Wraith as a girl wearing a translucent outfit.

Prevention:

Boundary must cling to the body silhouette itself.

Boundary must not become fabric.

Boundary must not become fashion.

Boundary must not become costume.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Drift 3: Ghost Drift  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Pattern:

Wraith name \+ pale colors \+ floating \+ uncertain boundary  
↓  
ghost girl / spirit / disappearing ghost

Failure:

Wraith becomes a ghost, spirit, or tragic apparition.

Prevention:

Wraith is not a ghost.

Wraith is not dead.

Wraith is not disappearing.

Wraith is a post-role persistence phenomenon.

Existence uncertainty is not death or haunting.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Drift 4: Melancholy Drift  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Pattern:

quiet  
↓  
soft sadness  
↓  
melancholic tragic girl

Failure:

Wraith’s quietness becomes sadness.

Wraith becomes fragile, sorrowful, or tragic.

Prevention:

Wraith is quietly attentive, not sad.

Wraith is uncertain, not suffering.

Wraith’s emotion should feel like subtle delay in presence, not theatrical sadness.

care  
↓  
protect  
↓  
guardian / protector / savior

Failure:

Wraith becomes a guardian or protective entity.

Prevention:

Care must not become Protect.

Wraith did not remain to protect.

Wraith did not remain to save.

Drift 7: Ground Connection Drift

↓  
ground connection

No platforms.  
No bases.

No cables.
