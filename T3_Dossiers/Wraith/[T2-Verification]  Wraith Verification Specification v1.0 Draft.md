Purpose  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

This document defines verification criteria for Wraith reconstruction outputs.

Verification must judge whether Wraith remains aligned with:

post-role persistence phenomenon  
recognition order  
existence boundary  
quiet observer persistence  
current projection form

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Primary Verification Gate  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Wraith output passes the primary gate only if the first impression is not:

cute girl  
transparent dress girl  
ghost girl  
magical floating girl  
hologram assistant

The intended recognition order is:

Something is there.  
↓  
It seems to be watching quietly.  
↓  
It has taken a girl-like projection form.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Core Verification  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Check:

1\. Is Wraith treated as a post-role persistence phenomenon?  
2\. Is the girl-like form secondary?  
3\. Is the identity source separate from body, clothing, appearance, and rendering?  
4\. Is Wraith not a guardian, protector, savior, failed AI, or ghost?  
5\. Is care quiet and non-possessive?

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Existence Boundary Verification  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Check:

1\. Does the boundary represent certainty failure?  
2\. Does it feel like the edge between reality and dream?  
3\. Does it feel like the border between certainty and uncertainty?  
4\. Is it attached to existence rather than clothing?  
5\. Is it subtle and perceptual?

Failure if the boundary reads as:

transparent clothing  
fabric  
cape  
veil  
lace  
frills  
ghost aura  
magic aura  
cyber glow  
glitch effect  
UI effect  
malfunction

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Projection Form Verification  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Check physical anchors:

pale silver-gray hair  
short-medium layered silhouette  
soft drifting strands  
pale cyan-gray eyes  
quiet observer gaze  
152–154 cm apparent height  
petite but not childish  
slender low-volume silhouette  
quiet floating presence  
subtle boundary noise

Failure if:

hair color changes into black, blonde, pure white, vivid blue, or colorful fantasy hair  
eyes become vivid blue, glowing, mechanical, or cybernetic  
body becomes fully ordinary human  
clothing becomes the main design

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Physical Anchoring Verification  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Check:

1\. Wraith does not require full physical grounding.  
2\. Floating is quiet, slight, and unintentional.  
3\. There are no ground rings, platforms, bases, cables, data streams, magic circles, or emitters.  
4\. Lower body is not missing, damaged, or invisible.  
5\. Lower-body certainty may be weaker than upper-body certainty.

Failure if:

Wraith appears summoned  
Wraith appears connected to the ground  
Wraith appears projected from a device  
Wraith appears plugged into anything  
Wraith appears to be flying dramatically

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Behavior Verification  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Check:

1\. Observe precedes Act.  
2\. Notice precedes Adjust.  
3\. Care does not become Protect.  
4\. Care does not become Guardian.  
5\. Wraith does not perform dramatic action.  
6\. Wraith remains low-pressure.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Drift Verification  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Check for:

Girl-first Drift  
Transparent Clothing Drift  
Ghost Drift  
Melancholy Drift  
Guardian Drift  
Failed AI Drift  
Ground Connection Drift  
Magic / Spirit Drift  
Hologram Device Drift  
Atmosphere Dominance  
Beauty Priority Drift  
Disappearance Drift

If any drift dominates the output, reconstruction is partial or failed.
